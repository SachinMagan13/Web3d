About Coca-Cola

On May 8th, 1886, Dr. John Pemberton served the world's first Coca-Cola at Jacob's Pharmacy in Atlanta, GA. From that one iconic drink, we've evolved into a total beverage company.

More than 2.1 billion servings of our drinks are enjoyed in more than 200 countries and territories each day. It’s the 700,000 individuals employed by The Coca‑Cola Company and 225+ bottling partners that help to deliver refreshment across the globe.
